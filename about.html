About Me

I’m a UX designer. Over the last 10 years I’ve worked at Geckoboard, Xero, and Mitsubishi Electric.

I have Masters degree in user centered design and a broad range of skills including user research, interface design, and prototyping. I’ve lead the design of major projects, working closely with creative teams, product managers and software developers. I love collaborating with others and sharing my passion for design.
